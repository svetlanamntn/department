The Department of Foreign Languages of the Lyceum of Creativity and Inventiveness "Prometeu Prim" is the largest of the 4 departments, including valuable experts in teaching English, French and German Languages. On the respective page you can meet mainly the teachers of Enlish, professionals with first or superior didactic degrees or Cambridge Certified.

The educational process in foreign languages department of the Lyceum "Prometeu-Prim" is carried out by 20 teachers: 9 English teachers, 9 French teachers and one German teacher. Teachers, with excellent theoretical and practical training, follow their professional vocation by guiding students in the mysteries of foreign language knowledge, develop their communication skills, critical thinking and creativity. Models of high professionalism and self-dedication, teachers are authors and co-authors of textbooks, guides, test booklets, student notebooks and other publications. The lyceum is also proud of the 5 English teachers, who managed to get CELTA Certified. Over the years, high school students have had the best results in English at the BAC exams, participate in prestigious international debate competitions, in international programs for the preparation of young leaders. The teachers of the Methodical Commission are national and international trainers, in high school, municipal and republican seminars on foreign languages which have been organized and conducted.
